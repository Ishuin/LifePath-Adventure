## 2026-05-20 - Adding Accessible Focus States\n**Learning:** Custom components like stylized navigation links and CTA buttons often lose clear visual hierarchy during keyboard navigation against complex animated backgrounds if relying solely on default browser outlines.\n**Action:** Consistently apply `:focus-visible` states that mirror `:hover` animations, combined with high-contrast outlines and `outline-offset` to ensure clear visibility against dark/animated backgrounds.
