## 2026-06-09 - [Feature Card Redundant Images]
**Learning:** [Screen readers will read the alt text on feature card images (e.g. "Explore") and then immediately read the adjacent h3 tags (e.g. "Explore"). This creates a redundant and annoying experience.]
**Action:** [Set alt="" and aria-hidden="true" on images that are visually illustrative but redundant to adjacent text headings.]
